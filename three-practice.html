What you're looking at. Every Three.js scene has the same skeleton: a Scene (the world), a Camera (your eye), a Renderer (draws it each frame), one or more Mesh objects (a shape + a surface material), and Lights so the surfaces read as 3D. An animate() loop redraws ~60 times a second, which is what makes the spin and your dragging feel live. Read the commented source to see each piece labeled.
