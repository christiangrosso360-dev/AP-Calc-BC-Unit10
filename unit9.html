Where to start

Two ways through

Learning it for the first time

Go in order. Parametric sets up the idea of a parameter driving a curve; Vectors is the same idea in motion language; then Polar graphing and Polar area build the other coordinate system from the ground up. Each tool animates the thing the textbook only describes.

Reviewing before a test

Drill the two things that lose points: the parametric motion problem (displacement vs. distance) and polar area (½∫r², and finding the right limits). The cheat sheet has every formula and the "which setup?" logic on one printable page.
